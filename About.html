This webpage is a little about me and my experiments with photography.

My Story
Ever since I started traveling around the world, I started exploring offbeat locations in search of tranquility. And in one such place arose my passion for photography. I fell in love with photography a long time ago. I was 13 when I first started taking photos. I captured everything. I was known as the friend who always carried a camera around.

But why? Why did I fall in love with photography?

I loved photography because it helped me show people the best things about themselves. I wanted to show the world in a different way that makes people think deeper. You see, photography is so much more than focusing and pressing a button. People who click photos capture the world in their own way. If 10 people take a photo of the same subject, I guarantee that the photo will look different from each person.

And thats how my journey with photography began. Head to the Portfolio page to check some of my work.
